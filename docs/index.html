Why hike with us

No fees, no paperwork. Just trails, fresh air, and good people.

Hikes for every level

From complete beginners to experienced hikers: every outing clearly states the difficulty, elevation gain, and estimated duration.

Transparent organisation

Meeting point, timings, and trail details shared in advance on Telegram. Sign-up polls handled directly by the bot.

Authentic Lazio landscapes

Lesser-known trails, nature reserves, medieval hill towns, and panoramas over the Roman countryside — well off the tourist circuit.

A genuine, non-profit community

An informal group of enthusiasts. The goal is to walk together — not to sell tours or collect memberships.
